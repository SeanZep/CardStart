How To Play!

Start with an Open & Curious Mind

It's important to approach the conversation with respectful and geniunely curious thoughts.

Truly Listen

A conversation should always be a two-way street. Take your time to truly listen and respond to others. Absorb what they’re sharing and then share your thoughts respectfully.

Be Present

Going along with truly listening, it’s best to put away all other distractions, such as phones. You want to be present in your conversation and the moment.

Take your Time to Answer

As mentioned, conversations are a two-way street. Take your time to think about your answer, and be honest when you do. It is when all participants slowly open up that you’ll make deeper connections.

Ways To Play!

Classic

Everyone takes turns to answer the card question on the screen.

Solo

Have a meaningful conversation with yourself. Use the questions as writing prompts for a journal entry or a vlog topic or simply a means of personal reflection.

Popcorn

One person answers the card question on the screen and then they popcorn to someone else who will answer the next card.

If I Were You

Pick a card, then have everyone else answer the question as if they were you. The person whose answer is closest to the truth wins the round.
